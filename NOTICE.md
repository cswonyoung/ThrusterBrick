## Note on patents (read this — it matters for "can't be suppressed")

CC0 waives copyright; it does **not** waive patent rights (see LICENSE §4a).
The thing that actually stops anyone from patenting this process and using that
patent to suppress it is **public disclosure itself**: once these documents are
publicly and verifiably published with a date, they are *prior art*. Prior art
cannot be un-published, and it blocks later patent claims over the same
disclosed subject matter. The timestamp (see `TIMESTAMPING.md`) is what fixes
that date beyond dispute.
